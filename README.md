## 🧪 What I Was Experimenting With

This isn't a project — it's a lab notebook. Here's what I was throwing at the wall:

- **Text tags** — headings (`h1`–`h6`), paragraphs, bold, italic, `<pre>` blocks
- **Semantic HTML** — `<main>`, `<section>`, `<article>`, `<aside>`, `<footer>`
- **Links & navigation** — external links (Google, YouTube), internal page links
- **Images** — inline images with `src`, `height`, `alt`
- **Tables** — with `<caption>`, `<thead>`, `<tbody>`, `colspan`
- **Forms** — text inputs, password fields, radio buttons, checkboxes, `<select>`, `<textarea>`, submit
- **Subscript & superscript** — H₂O and 3² = 9 energy
- **Embedded media** — YouTube `<iframe>`, `<video>` tag
- **Multi-page structure** — linking between `index.html` and `hellofolder/hello.html`

---

## 💡 Notes to Future Me

- The `<h1>` inside `<head>` doesn't render — it belongs in `<body>`. You'll laugh at this later.
- Duplicate `id` attributes on form elements will bite you when you hit JavaScript.
- `<pre>` is for preformatted text, not layout hacks — but hey, you figured that out.
- That empty `<video src="">` was optimistic. Love it.
